## 3) E2E Test Cases

### TC-E01: TOC Smooth Scroll
- **Preconditions**: Blog detail page with long content and multiple headings
- **Steps**:
  1. Navigate to `/blog/[slug]`

- **Expected**: Page scrolls smoothly to the 3rd heading and that heading is visible at the top (with scroll-margin)
- **Test file**: `e2e/blog-detail.spec.ts`

### TC-E02: Reading Progress Sync
- **Preconditions**: Blog detail page loaded
- **Steps**:

- **Expected**: ReadingProgressBar width increases proportionally to scroll depth
- **Test file**: `e2e/blog-detail.spec.ts`
